## Venue Details

### 1. Quantitative Finance (Taylor & Francis)
- **Website:** https://www.tandfonline.com/journals/rquf20
- **Scope:** Mathematical and computational methods in finance
- **Review time:** ~3-6 months
- **Pro:** Strong fit for factor investing + uncertainty quantification
- **Con:** No special issue, regular submission

### 2. Journal of Financial Data Science (PM Research)
- **Website:** https://www.pm-research.com/content/iijjfds
- **Scope:** AI, ML, data science in investment management
- **Review time:** ~2-4 months
- **Pro:** Practitioner + academic audience, fast review
- **Con:** Lower IF (1.0)

### 3. Journal of Risk and Financial Management (MDPI)
- **Website:** https://www.mdpi.com/journal/jrfm
- **Scope:** Risk management, financial modeling
- **Review time:** ~1-2 months (fast)
- **Pro:** Already published conformal prediction paper (Fantazzini 2024)
- **Con:** Open access fees (~$2000), lower prestige
